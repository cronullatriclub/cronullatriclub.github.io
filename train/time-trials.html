CTC Time Trials

Come take part in our exciting time trial series, where you'll experience the thrill of practicing time trials in a unique format designed for all levels.

Event Details

Training Info

Club Members Only: only registered AusTri club members can participate

Special Rules: no passing allowed on the bike through the transition zone - no personal music devices allowed during the session

Check-in Process

1. SIGN UP online (prior to race morning). Registrations close at 5pm the Friday night before the Time Trial, and the registration page is capped at 30 riders

2. Check-in

3. Self-seed starting with slower riders starting at the front, riders expected to finish in more than 30 mins start first

4. Riders set off at 30 sec intervals starting at 7 AM
