Event Details

Wednesday Junior Club Run

On every other Wednesday. Taken by one of our Junior coaches with the aim to develop fundamental running skills and build confidence in a supportive environment.

What to Expect

Arrive at the venue 10 minutes before the start time. The run will likely be around 5km, bring warm clothes and a headlight.

Time

Starts 6:20pm - wraps up around 7:30pm

Contact

For additional information, reach out to juniors@cronullatriclub.com.au
