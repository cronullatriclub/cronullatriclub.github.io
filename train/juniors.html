Program Details

Cronulla Triathlon Club Juniors

Cronulla Triathlon Club has one of the strongest and most supportive Junior programs in NSW, catering for athletes from 5 years old right through to Youth and Junior competitors.

Our program is designed to be fun, inclusive and rewarding – building confidence and friendships while developing triathlon skills. With expert coaches, loan equipment options, and clear pathways into higher-level competition, every junior has the chance to reach their potential.

Weekly Sessions

Training Program

Our juniors train across swim, bike, run, and transitions under accredited coaches throughout the Summer and Winter seasons.

Winter Schedule

Monday – Bike Session High School Athletes only 5:30–6:30pm · Sutherland Cycle Track
⚠️ Winter bike sessions are exclusively for high school athletes due to low-light and weather conditions – safety first. Additional race opportunities are shared on the Cronulla Juniors Facebook page.

Summer Schedule

Monday – Bike Session Ages 5–11 & 12+ 5:30–6:30pm · Sutherland Cycle Track
Wednesday – Swim/Run All ages 6:30–7:30pm · Cronulla Triathlon Clubhouse
Saturday – Ocean Swim Youth A, B & Juniors (proficiency required) 7:30am · South Cronulla/Kurnell/Gunnamatta
