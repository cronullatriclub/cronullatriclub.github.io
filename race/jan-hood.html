The Jan Hood Trophy

Named after one of our life members this annual award goes to our fastest male and female athletes to cross the finishing line at the Sutherland2Surf.

Rules

  • You must be wearing Cronulla Triathlon Club gear during the race.
  • No other clothing worn can bear political or religious statements.
  • You must be a current Cronulla Triathlon Club member.
  • You must have an official race entry to the Sutherland2Surf and abide by their official race rules.
