CTC Duathlon Series

Come take part in our exciting duathlon series, where you'll experience the thrill of both running and cycling in a unique format designed for all levels.

Event Details

Schedule

6:00pm: Registration kicks off - grab your timing chip, race number, and pay.

6:10pm: Get briefed on the race - it's time to strategize!

6:15pm: Race start - unleash your speed as you sprint into action.

7:30pm: Close out the event - but not before you've left it all on the track!

Special Rules

  • There is no passing allowed on bike through the transition zone
  • All bikes must be fitted with functional front and rear lights for non Daylight Savings races.
  • Participants will not be allowed to use personal music devices during the race
  • This is a non-drafting event. Due to the short course, we enforce a 7-metre draft zone and a maximum of 15 seconds to pass through the draft zone.

Race Fee

  • CTC Members Free!!
  • Non CTC TA members $10
  • Non TA members $10 + $5 (One Day Licence)
