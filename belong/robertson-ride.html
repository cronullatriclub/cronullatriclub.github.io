Event Details

Sutherland to Robertson Pub Annual Ride

Endurance. Elevation. Epic club day out. One of our signature rides of the year is back.

A classic test of fitness, pacing, and grit - with great company, stunning scenery, and the legendary Macquarie Pass finish. Whether you’re building endurance, chasing a challenge, or just keen for a cracking day on the bike with the club, this ride delivers.

Ride Details

Start: Sutherland Bike Track

Start Time

Pack B 5.45am MEET

Pack A 6am MEET

(both packs be ready to leave shortly after meet time)

Finish Line

Robertson Pub (for well deserved food and drinks)

Crown the King & Queen of the Mountain

Route & Stops

Sutherland → Bald Hill

Water stop & group photo

Bald Hill → Wollongong

Breakfast stop

Wollongong → Albion Park

Water Stop

Albion Park → Bottom of Macquarie Pass

Regroup before the climb

Macquarie Pass → Robertson Pub (for food and drinks)

main event — climb strong and earn your crown!

Ride Groups

A & B Groups from Sutherland to Wollongong

From Wollongong onwards, we ride together as one group

Supportive, well-managed, and club-focused

Ride Options

✔ Sutherland → Wollongong & return

✔ Full Sutherland → Robertson Pub ride

Choose the option that suits your fitness and experience.

Know your capability — this is a long day with a serious climb. There is no pressure to do the full ride — both options are respected and encouraged.
