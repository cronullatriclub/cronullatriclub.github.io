Junior Bike Skills

Join us for an exhilarating bike skills session every Monday at 5:30pm at the Waratah Bike Track, located at Waratah Park, Rawson Avenue, Sutherland. Because of the low-light and winter conditions, this session is only for ages 14+.

What to Expect

Gain confidence and enhance your biking skills under the guidance of experienced instructors. Don’t forget to come prepared in your cycling gear, helmet and a serviced bike for a safe and enjoyable experience. Stay hydrated by bringing along a drink and ensure visibility with front and rear lights. Bring your running shoes for transition practice.

Time

Starts 5:30pm - wraps up around 6:30pm
